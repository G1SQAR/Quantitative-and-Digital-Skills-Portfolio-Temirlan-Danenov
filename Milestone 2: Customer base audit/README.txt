Milestone 2: Customer base audit
-----------------------------------------
Conducted decile and cohort analysis over time to evaluate the client portfolio. 
Used the Pareto principle to determine which aspects generate the most profit. 
I found that 10% of clients account for approximately 40% of the company's revenue. 
Calculated and interpreted complex metrics such as retention and churn rates, average order frequency, and average order value. 
I used Excel pivot tables to structure the data and create graphs reflecting the behavioral patterns of different cohorts. 
Based on the analysis, I provided recommendations for optimizing marketing resources and managing the customer lifecycle.
